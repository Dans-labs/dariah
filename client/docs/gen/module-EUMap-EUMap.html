EUMap

EUMap~ EUMap

stateless, DOM-modifying Component

An ordinary faceted filter on the country field plus a container for the map, with life cycle methods to place and update markers when the filter results have changed.

componentDidMount(filterSettings, filteredAmountOthers, amounts`, countriesMap) → {DOM}

Source:
  • object/EUMap.jsx, line 159

After the initial mount we get the country data and perform initializations that depend on that information:

  • we put the map in place
  • we put the country boundaries on the pane
  • we add markers, style them, and maintain references to them

These do not have to be repeated after subsequent updates.
